The Announcement You've All Been Waiting For

 Feelingloved2 29 days ago

Before I start off the post, I just want to take some time and thank you guys again for continuing to show up and play on our beta release of Practice & Bunkers.

I know we’ve been pretty quiet lately, and we apologize for that, but we’ve been working on a lot of stuff behind the scenes. We’re getting ready for our Season 1 launch which will include a stat reset for all minigames along with the release of new games as well. More information on the specific changes for each server will be posted in the coming days.

I’ve seen a lot of players asking which server is coming first; Teams or Squads. The answer to that is Squads. We want to ensure that there are no major issues when we launch Teams. Squads will be our first map that is running all of the updated code and infrastructure. We want the user experience to be seamless. We do not have a confirmed date for SOTW quite yet, but we are confident that we can release it this month.

Now… let me address the elephant in the room: “What about the cheaters!?”

This game has a lot of potential to be fun and exciting to play, like it once was in the past, but in order to do that, we have to ensure that there are as little cheaters on the network as possible. It is no surprise that a majority of the players that showed up to our pre-release on Practice were cheating, and we're aware of that. There is only so much that a server-side anticheat can do to combat every single one of these cheaters, especially when they can just log back on an alt account.

The reason we’ve gone so long without updates is because we wanted to ensure we had a way to fight this problem.

In the coming days, we will be rolling out our very own client; VolixPvP Client! It will include a simple anticheat, various mods and optimizations that will likely double your normal FPS. VolixPvP Client will be required to log onto most servers on VolixPvP. Depending on how many people adopt the use of the client in the first month or so, we will consider making it a requirement to join the entire network. Our team has spent the last few weeks working hard to ensure that we stop a majority of the cheaters. With that being said, it will not stop 100% of the people cheating, but we’re going to be working around the clock in order to mitigate as many attack vectors as possible. Along with the FPS boost, mods, and anticheat, we also can integrate a lot of server-side features that is only possible with a client. We will be announcing those features on the server-specific announcement.

For more information and some screenshots, visit https://VolixPvP.com/client.

It is not yet released, but we plan to start rolling it out in the coming days. Thank you all for the continuous support and we’re sorry it took a while to address the cheating situation. I hope we make it up to you <3.

Weekend Updates, EU Practice & Bunkers

 Griffin 1 month ago

Before I start off the post, I just wanted to let you all know that we are aware of the cheating situation. It is no surprise that the server is filled with cheaters, as is every other server with no client-side anticheat (spoiler alert: all of them). We will be addressing this issue as soon as we can.

Now onto the updates:

Last night we performed some more infrastructure work that allowed us to scale up on Bunkers servers so multiple games can run at once. We also got the EU Proxy and Practice server up and running. Keep in mind that if you are European and want to see a ping difference, you must connect to eu.VolixPvP.com and to the EU Practice server. Both of those should now be online.

Once again, thank you all for the continuing support, and we hope you enjoy!

Information Regarding Today's Downtime, KitMap, & Bunkers

 macguy8 1 month ago

From roughly 11PM ET last night to 10PM ET today, the network was whitelisted while we performed some much needed infrastructure work. As many of you noticed, the connection to the network was not as good as we would have liked, especially for our European players. We made the decision to move our servers to a more central location, with a higher quality network. This should result in lower latency and greater stability for all of our players. Unfortunately, we cannot get the EU proxy and practice server up right now, but it should be available shortly.

In the meantime, we have decided to go ahead and release KitMap, with some changes:
• You can now do /challenges to see a list of daily challenges that change every 24 hours. Once you complete a challenge, you will receive a key that you can redeem at spawn.
• Claiming is enabled starting at (500, 500)
• Building is enabled starting at (300, 300)
• DTR loss outside of warzone is 0.25 so factions who actually want to play KitMap and practice their HCF skills have a chance at going raidable.

We will also start limited testing of Bunkers. These games will be hosted by staff and occur manually during this testing period. More information with changes and updates will be posted in the coming week.

In addition to these changes, we're now allowing players on Minecraft 1.8.x to join the network. 1.8 support is currently in beta, so please report any issues to https://VolixPvP.com/bugs

And finally, we'd like to say thank you to everyone who joined VolixPvP and helped us get things stabilized during this very first week. In the coming week, we plan to talk more about HCSquads, HCTeams, and cheaters. Keep your eyes peeled. 👀
